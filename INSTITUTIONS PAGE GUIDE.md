### **PILLAR 3: PLACEMENT PROBABILITY (PP) INDEX**
**What It Is:**
Data-driven predictive system that identifies students at risk of remaining unplaced and provides targeted interventions.

**How It Works:**

**Stage 1: Data Collection (Semester 5)**
Collect multi-dimensional data on every student:

**Academic Performance:**
- CGPA, semester-wise trends
- Subject-wise performance
- Practical vs theory gap
- Project quality

**Technical Skills Assessment:**
- Coding assessment (HackerRank/LeetCode style)
- System design basics
- Problem-solving ability
- Technology knowledge

**Aptitude & Reasoning:**
- Quantitative aptitude
- Logical reasoning
- Verbal ability

**Soft Skills Assessment:**
- Communication (English speaking, writing)
- Presentation skills
- Teamwork and collaboration
- Leadership potential

**Psychometric Assessment:**
- Personality traits
- Learning agility
- Stress handling
- Career aspirations

---

**Stage 2: PP Index Calculation (Proprietary Algorithm)**

Each student gets a **Placement Probability Score (0-100%)**

**Algorithm considers:**
- Historical placement data from your college
- Industry hiring patterns
- Technology demand trends
- Similar student profiles who got placed/not placed

**Student Segmentation:**

**GREEN (80-100%):** High probability of placement
- Strong across all dimensions
- Minimal intervention needed
- Focus: Premium company preparation

**YELLOW (60-79%):** Medium probability
- Good in some areas, weak in others
- Moderate intervention needed
- Focus: Targeted skill building

**ORANGE (40-59%):** At risk
- Significant gaps in multiple areas
- Intensive intervention needed
- Focus: Foundation strengthening + specific weaknesses

**RED (0-39%):** High risk of remaining unplaced
- Critical gaps across dimensions
- Emergency intervention required
- Focus: Intensive remedial training + alternative career counseling

---

**Stage 3: Targeted Interventions (Semester 6-8)**

**For GREEN students:**
- Advanced topics training
- System design preparation
- Product company interview prep
- Leadership development

**For YELLOW students:**
- Specific skill gap training
- Extra coding practice
- Communication workshops
- Additional mock interviews

**For ORANGE students:**
- Intensive coding bootcamp
- Foundation strengthening
- One-on-one mentoring
- Daily practice and monitoring

**For RED students:**
- Emergency intervention program
- Alternative career paths exploration
- Remedial training (if time permits)
- Realistic expectation setting

---

**Stage 4: Continuous Monitoring**
- Monthly PP Index update
- Track improvement trajectories
- Adjust interventions based on progress
- Early warning system for deterioration

**Visual Dashboard showing:**
