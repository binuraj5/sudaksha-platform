# PROMPT: Industry-Specific Training Solutions Section
## For: sudaksha.com/corporates page

---

## CONTEXT

You are building a **React/Next.js section component** called `IndustryTrainingSolutions` for Sudaksha's `/corporates` page. Sudaksha is a B2B corporate training company in India offering outcome-driven, precision-curated workforce training.

The existing page (`sudaksha.com/corporates`) uses a dark-professional theme with:
- Dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f, #111118)
- Accent color: Electric blue (#00d4ff) and gold (#f5a623)
- Font stack: professional sans-serif
- Card-based grid layouts, glassmorphism cards
- CTA buttons with gradient borders

---

## SECTION GOAL

Replace the current minimal industry grid (which only shows industry names + links) with a **rich, interactive Industry-Specific Training Solutions section** that:

1. Shows **12 industries** as selectable tabs/cards
2. For each selected industry, displays **5 training category tabs**: Domain-Specific | Technology | Behavioural | Cognitive | Specialist
3. Under each category tab, lists **8–12 specific training programs**
4. Shows **"Solutions in Demand"** — a highlighted sub-panel showing top 3–4 trending/high-demand programs for that industry
5. Has a CTA at the bottom: "Design Your [Industry] Training Program →"

---
